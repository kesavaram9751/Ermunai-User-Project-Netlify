Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Ermunai Organic Farm Foods?
Ermunai Organic Farm Foods is a Chennai-based brand offering organic millet mixes, health powders, and ready-to-cook traditional foods without preservatives.
2. Are Ermunai Organic products organic and natural?
Yes. All our food mixes are 100% natural, made from native grains, with no artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives.
3. What products do you offer?
We provide organic millet mixes, instant dosa/idli mixes, upma mix, pongal mix, health powders, and podis made from traditional recipes.
4. Are your millet mixes good for health?
Yes. Millets are rich in fiber, protein, and minerals. They support digestion, manage weight, and are diabetic-friendly.
5. Are your products gluten-free?
Most millet-based mixes are naturally gluten-free. Please check each product’s ingredient list for details.
6. How do I cook your instant mixes?
Just add hot water or cook with simple ingredients as per pack instructions. Ready in minutes – perfect for breakfast, dinner, or travel.
7. Are your products suitable for children and elders?
Yes. Our mixes are safe, healthy, and made with gentle native ingredients, ideal for all age groups.
8. Do you deliver across India?
Yes. We ship to all major cities and towns in India within 3–7 working days. International delivery is also available on request.
9. How can I order your products?
You can order directly from our website or contact us at +91 9840752833 for bulk and retail orders.
10. Is your packaging eco-friendly?
Yes. We use eco-conscious and sustainable packaging to reduce plastic and support a greener lifestyle.
