Our Story

At Ermunai Organic Farm Foods, we believe food is more than just taste — it is tradition, health, and a way of life. Inspired by the wisdom of our ancestors, we bring back the richness of native rice, millets, and herbs in a convenient ready-to-cook form.


In today’s fast lifestyle, people often compromise health for convenience. Our mission is to change that — by creating instant mixes that are not only easy to prepare but also carry the authentic flavors and nutrition of traditional foods. Every product is made with care, using clean ingredients and eco-friendly packaging, so that you and your family can enjoy natural wellness every day.

Our Mission

We craft ready-to-cook traditional rice, millet, and herbal mixes using authentic native ingredients, ensuring convenience without compromise on health. Through eco-friendly packaging and sustainable sourcing, we aim to make organic living accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To revive traditional food culture with healthy, convenient, and eco-friendly food mixes, making every Indian household choose natural wellness over processed fast foods.

Our Values
