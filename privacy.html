This Privacy Policy describes how **M/s. Ermunai Organic Farm Foods** (referred to as "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit, use, or make a purchase from our website (the "Site" or "Service").

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information for different purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

A. Personal Information (PII)

This includes information that can be used to identify you directly when you register or place an order:

  • Identity Data: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Contact Data: Billing address, shipping address, and delivery instructions.
  • Account Data: Username, password (encrypted), and account preferences.

B. Financial Information (Collected by Payment Gateway)

  • We use third-party payment gateways (like Razorpay) to process all payments.
  • We **DO NOT** store your credit card, debit card, or net banking details on our servers. This sensitive financial data is collected, processed, and stored securely by our payment gateway partners, and they are responsible for its security.
  • We only receive payment confirmation, amount, and limited transaction details (like the Order ID).

C. Usage Data and Cookies

We automatically collect information when you access the Service ("Usage Data").

  • Log Data: Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, operating system, pages of our Service that you visit, time and date of your visit.
  • Tracking Data (Cookies): We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service (e.g., maintaining items in your shopping cart) and enhance your user experience. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following essential purposes:

  • To **Process and Fulfill Your Orders** and manage payments.
  • To deliver organic food products to your specified address through our logistics partners.
  • To maintain and improve our Service (e.g., monitor usage, analyze customer trends).
  • To manage your Account and provide customer support.
  • To send you transactional emails (order confirmations, delivery updates).
  • To send you promotional offers and updates (if you have explicitly opted-in during registration).
  • To prevent and address technical issues or fraudulent activity.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your Personal Information. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: We share necessary data with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business:

  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose your Personal Information if required to do so by Indian law, in response to a court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of **Ermunai Organic Farm Foods** or others.

The security of your data is paramount.
